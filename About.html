About

Hello, I'm Jani.

A Polish artist and graphic designer based in Rotterdam, Netherlands, currently studying at Willem de Kooning Academy.

I approach creative work through a digital lens — with a focus on clarity, structure, and simplicity. My process is driven by a desire to make ideas visually coherent and thoughtfully organised. I specialise in digital art and creative web design, while always exploring other media and learning new ways of making.

I believe graphic design shapes our lives — it's everywhere, influencing how people move through spaces and interact with the world.
