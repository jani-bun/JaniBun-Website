About


Hello and welcome, I’m Jani. I am a Polish artist based in Rotterdam, Netherlands, currently studying graphic design at Willem de Kooning Academy .


I am a designer who approaches creative work through a digital lens, focusing on clarity, structure, and simplicity; my process is driven by a desire to make ideas visually coherent and thoughtfully organised. In my work I’m especially focusing on digital art and creative web design, while also always exploring other media and learning new ways of making. I believe graphic design shapes our lives — it’s everywhere, influencing how people move through spaces and interact with the world. It’s a constant and ever-evolving source of inspiration.


You can reach me at janbunka0@gmail.com for any inquiries, and folow me on my socials - thank you!
